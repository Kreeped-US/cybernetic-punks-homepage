# CREATOR STRATEGY — staged roadmap (high-level map)

> Captured at high level by request. The point: the creator plays aren't four
> options to pick — they're four SIZE-GATED STAGES that build on each other.
> Detail each stage when it actually comes up (esp. Stage 1's offer specifics).

## The frame
Growth thesis = community engagement > features (existing principle). Creators
are the highest-leverage community node. So a structured creator strategy is
aligned with the real growth lever — but it scales IN STAGES with site size.
Draw the whole arc now (cheap); build each stage when its gate opens.

## The 4 stages (gated by size/maturity)

### Stage 1 — NOW: Trusted Contributor Package (play "B")
- **What:** a defined offer — trusted high-level players/creators supply
  verified intel/observations; get CREDIT + free premium + a badge/role +
  a submission path. (Formalizes the existing LordTT/neodeye precedent.)
- **Why now / why small-is-fine:** relationship-based, not audience-based —
  doesn't need traffic. Works BETTER small.
- **Quadruple duty:** (1) IT IS the verification CONFIRMED-mechanism (the open
  question from the verification project — who confirms stats in-game), (2)
  feeds the AI moat (the #6 "verified contributor knowledge" layer from the
  AI-quality roadmap), (3) gives creators real status, (4) credited
  contributors naturally talk about what they're credited on → seed growth.
- **Build note:** mostly a defined offer + relationship, NOT infrastructure.
  The only light build (grant free premium + badge + submission path) waits on
  monetization ENFORCEMENT existing (so "grant premium" means something).
  Relationship-first; productize lightly later.

### Stage 2 — SOON-ISH (as product matures): Creator Tools (play "C")
- **What:** shareable build links, embeddable tier-lists/graphics, a creator
  dashboard, co-branded loadout links creators share with their audience.
- **Why:** turns creators into a DISTRIBUTION channel — every shared link is a
  CyberneticPunks impression. Borrows THEIR audience; doesn't need ours.
- **Gate:** viable once there are tools worth sharing → tied to the AI-QUALITY
  ROADMAP (better, deeper AI output = more shareable). Stage-1 contributors
  are the first natural users.

### Stage 3 — WHEN SOME AUDIENCE: Traded Promotion (play "A")
- **What:** free premium + featured/contributor status in exchange for
  coverage (not paid sponsorship — traded value).
- **Gate:** works once there's something worth featuring + enough credibility
  that coverage isn't a favor. Lands better after B + C give a real product +
  contributor roster to point at.

### Stage 4 — WHEN REAL TRAFFIC: Paid Sponsorship / Ads (play "D")
- **What:** brands/creators pay US for placement, sponsored spotlights, etc.
  The eventual REVENUE layer (beyond subscriptions).
- **Gate:** genuinely PREMATURE now — nobody pays to advertise to pre-traction
  traffic. Documented only so we don't build in ways that preclude it. Park.
- **Note:** also reconciles with the platform's no-ads-degrading-trust
  posture — any future ads must not compromise the verified/honest brand.

## How it weaves into existing threads (why it's not new scope)
- **Verification project:** Stage 1 (B) IS the open CONFIRMED-mechanism,
  productized. Same people, same trust model.
- **AI-quality roadmap:** Stage 1 feeds the #6 contributor-knowledge layer;
  Stage 2 is gated by how shareable the AI output is (roadmap quality).
- **Monetization:** every stage grants "free premium" → depends on the tier
  system + enforcement existing. Creators get comped tiers; the tiers must
  exist first.
- So the creator strategy is a LENS connecting verification + AI-quality +
  monetization, not a separate build.
